The Firm

A multi-family office, evolved into a co-investment platform.

Black Pearl Capital was established as a multi-family office to manage the proprietary investments of its founding partners. It has since evolved into a co-investment platform specialising in opportunistic private and public equity across industry sectors.

Headquartered in Geneva with offices across four jurisdictions, the firm invests globally — spanning the United States, Europe, Asia and the Middle East — with a preference for control positions and patient, long-term ownership.

Our global network is the engine of the firm — identifying opportunities, allocating capital efficiently, executing swiftly and nurturing the management teams that steward it. Black Pearl Capital

Track Record

A history of disciplined investing.

2007

The firm is established

Black Pearl Capital is founded as a multi-family office, making its first real estate commitments and beginning the deployment of proprietary capital.

2009

Early-mover in renewables

The firm invests ahead of the market in solar, wind and biomass projects, later divesting and successfully realising the portfolio.

2011

Industrial control investment

Acquisition of Canadian Energy Equipment Manufacturing (CEEM), a UAE-based specialist fabricator serving oil and gas operators across the MENA region.

2015

Operational turnaround realised

Following a hands-on restructuring and on-the-ground operational support, the firm exits its UAE industrial fabrication platform.

2016

Entry into hyperscale data centres

First development-equity co-investments in hyperscale data centre projects across the USA and Europe.

2020

Scaling industrial manufacturing

International Dominican Aluminium expands into a regional extrusion hub serving the Caribbean and US markets through a major joint venture.

2023

Co-investment platform formalised

Launch of Black Pearl Capital Partners SPC, a CIMA-registered private fund, opening high-conviction strategies to external co-investors.

Today

A global platform

More than $500 million deployed across 30+ portfolio companies, with $15 billion in aggregate development value and a network spanning four jurisdictions.

Stewarding capital across cycles, generations and borders — with discipline.
