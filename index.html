The Firm

A multi-family office, evolved into a co-investment platform.

Established in 2007 to manage the proprietary investments of its founding partners, Black Pearl Capital has grown into a co-investment platform specialising in opportunistic private and public equity across industry sectors and geographies.

Headquartered in Geneva with offices across four jurisdictions, the firm invests globally with a preference for control positions and patient, long-term ownership.
